USAID Involvement in the Carribean

The USAID is responsible for the US governments contribution to civilian aid abroad. These contributions are known as an economic assistance to the nations that receive US funding. An example of a project that is funded through this branch of the government is the Feed the Future program that partners with UF in Haiti in an attempt to increase sustainable agriculture throughout Haiti.
